      Roofen Cookie Policy

      This Cookie Policy applies to this website and explains how cookies, localStorage, and similar technologies may be used when you browse Roofen.

      1. Introduction

      Roofen is an independent roofing contractor matching platform. We help homeowners submit roofing requests and compare local provider options. Cookies help us keep the website functional, responsive, and easier to use.

      2. What Cookies Are

      Cookies are small files or data items stored on your device by your browser. Similar technologies, such as localStorage, may store small pieces of information in your browser to remember preferences or support website functionality.

      3. Types of Cookies and Similar Technologies We May Use

      This website may use different types of cookies or similar technologies depending on the features enabled and how you interact with the website.

      • Essential cookies: support basic website functionality, security, navigation, and preference storage.
      • Preference cookies: remember choices such as cookie banner acceptance or cancellation.
      • Analytics cookies: may help us understand website visits, page performance, and general user behavior.
      • Marketing or advertising cookies: may be used if advertising, retargeting, or measurement tools are added to the website.

      4. Cookie Banner and Local Storage

      Roofen includes a cookie banner with Accept and Cancel buttons. When you select an option, your choice may be saved in your browser using localStorage so the banner does not continue appearing on every page visit.

      Clearing browser data, using a different browser, using private browsing, or using another device may reset this preference.

      5. Why We Use Cookies

      Cookies and similar technologies may be used for the following purposes:

      • To remember your cookie banner choice.
      • To support navigation and website functionality.
      • To understand how visitors use the website.
      • To improve page layout, performance, content, and user experience.
      • To support security, debugging, and operational needs.
      • To support advertising or measurement if those tools are later enabled.

      6. Third-Party Cookies and Tools

      Some cookies may be placed by third-party services if the website uses analytics, advertising, embedded tools, fonts, scripts, or other external services. These third parties may collect information according to their own policies.

      Roofen does not control third-party cookie practices. Review the privacy and cookie policies of third-party services for more information about their data practices.

      7. Cookies and Quote Requests

      Cookies are separate from the information you submit through the quote request form. Form information may include contact details, ZIP code, service type, urgency, and project message. To learn more about how submitted information may be used, review the Privacy Policy.

      8. Your Cookie Choices

      You can control cookies in several ways. Some settings may affect website functionality or prevent certain features from working as expected.

      • You may click Accept or Cancel in the cookie banner where available.
      • You may block or delete cookies through your browser settings.
      • You may clear localStorage and browsing data through your browser tools.
      • You may use browser privacy settings or extensions to limit tracking.
      • You may choose not to use the website if you do not agree with cookie usage.

      9. Browser Controls

      Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete cookies, or receive alerts before cookies are stored. Browser controls vary, so review your browser’s help section for instructions specific to your browser and device.

      10. Updates to This Cookie Policy

      Roofen may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Continued use of the website after updates are posted means you accept the updated Cookie Policy.
