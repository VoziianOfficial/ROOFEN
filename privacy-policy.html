      Roofen Privacy Policy

      This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through this website and related quote request interactions. By using this website, you agree to the practices described below.

      1. Introduction

      Roofen is an independent platform that helps homeowners request roofing service options from local independent providers. Roofen does not perform roofing work directly and does not guarantee the work of any contractor or provider.

      2. Information We May Collect

      We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you use the website, submit a quote request, contact us, or interact with site features.

      • Contact information, such as name, email address, phone number, and ZIP code.
      • Roofing project information, such as service type, urgency, roof issue, and message details.
      • Communication information, such as emails, calls, or messages sent to Roofen.
      • Technical information, such as browser type, device information, pages visited, and general usage data.
      • Cookie-related information, depending on your browser settings and consent choices.

      3. How We May Use Information

      We may use collected information to operate the website, process quote requests, communicate with users, and improve the platform experience.

      • To help connect homeowners with independent local service providers.
      • To respond to quote requests, contact messages, and support questions.
      • To organize requests by service type, location, urgency, and project details.
      • To improve website functionality, design, security, and user experience.
      • To comply with applicable legal, regulatory, or operational requirements.

      4. Sharing Information With Independent Providers

      When you submit a quote request, your information may be shared with independent local service providers, contractor networks, or service partners that may contact you about your roofing project.

      These providers are independent third parties. Roofen does not control how independent providers perform roofing work and does not warrant or guarantee provider services. Homeowners are responsible for verifying contractor license, insurance, qualifications, estimates, and project terms before hiring.

      5. Service Providers and Business Operations

      We may share information with vendors or service providers that help operate the website, manage communications, host data, analyze website performance, or support business operations. These parties may access information only as needed to provide services to Roofen.

      6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

      This website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve user experience, remember cookie preferences, understand site usage, and support website performance. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

      For more information about cookie usage on this website, review the Cookie Policy.

      7. Analytics and Website Improvement

      We may use analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with the website. This may include pages viewed, time spent on pages, general location signals, device type, browser type, and other usage information.

      8. Your Choices

      You can choose not to submit personal information through the website. You may also adjust cookie preferences in your browser or contact Roofen with privacy-related requests.

      • You may decline non-essential cookies through the cookie banner where available.
      • You may disable cookies in your browser settings.
      • You may contact us to ask questions about information submitted through the website.
      • You may choose not to hire any contractor or provider that contacts you.

      9. Data Security

      We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to help protect information. However, no website, system, or transmission method can be guaranteed completely secure.

      10. Third-Party Links

      This website may contain links to third-party websites or services. Roofen is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of third-party websites. Review third-party policies before submitting information to them.

      11. Children’s Privacy

      This website is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through this website, please contact us.

      12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

      Roofen may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Continued use of the website after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
